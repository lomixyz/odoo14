SaaS Kit Trial

Allowing The User of Odoo SaaS Kit to provide Trial instance to their clients and converts the Trial into Subscription as per their requirements.

Salient Features

  • Client Can Purchase and Use The Trial version of Odoo instance provided by your SaaS.
  • Can Provide Trial of any Plan.
  • Auto and Manual Payment reminder for Client after Expiration of Trial Period.
  • Client can convert the Trial instance into Subscription at any time before or after Expiration of Trial by Purchasing Subscription.
  • Client Have option to Purchase the same instance or to Purchase the New Instance after using the Trial.

Setup& Configuration

Trial Product Configuration

This module depends upon another module "Odoo SaaS Kit."

You can download the module from link: https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/13.0/odoo_saas_kit/

After the successful installation of both the modules, you can configure the Trial settings from plan whom Trial you want to provide to your clients.

Create a Trial Product.


Add Trial Product Plan

  • Trial Period: Enter Trial Period in No. of Days.

  • Trial Product: Select the Trial Product which you have created in previous Step for Providing Trial.

Auto Purchase Reminder of Trial: Select to Active the Auto Purchase Reminder of Trial Expirations.

Work Flow.

  • After successful configuration of Plan and Trial Product, Plan Product will get a Link Get XX Days/Day Trial By clicking on that user will get the Trial Instance.

  • Trial Contract will be created at Admin's end which will get the Trial batch with Trial Period Details.

  • When Trial get Expired then related Contract will get the Expired batch.

  • Client can Purchase the Subscription from its Account Portal.

  • Client have options to Purchase the subscription of same Instance of Trial or to create a new Instance from same Plan.

  • If Client Purchase the Subscription for same Trial Instance then Related Contract will get the Trial Period Details.

  • If Client Purchased Subscription for New Instance then Old Contract will get the Details of new Instance's contract with Trial Converted Batch.

  • Client will also get the Countdown clock and Purchase Link while using the Trial version.

  • Client Instance after Expiration of Trial
