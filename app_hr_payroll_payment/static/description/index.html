Appness Payroll Payment

Appness Payroll Payment allows you to create payments from within the payroll batch.

A new state ‘Paid’ is added to the payroll batch to indicate whether the batch was paid or not.

Create a payroll batch and confirm it. After posting the entry coming from the payroll batch, you may do payment.

A payment is automatically created with the selected payment journal

Ability to view the payment(s) related to a single payroll batch

Ability to select whether the payments are made per employee or for all employees in the batch
